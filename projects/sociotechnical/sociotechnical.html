What is Sociotechnical Architecture?

Sociotechnical Architecture is about taking an holistic co-design approach to technical and organizational systems, given the inherent impact they have on each other.

In other words (from Nick Tune): When we are architecting a software system, we must consider the impact on the teams in the organization and vice-versa.

(inspired by Nick Tune's Sociotechnical Systems Evolution loop)
