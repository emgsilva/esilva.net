  • Everyone Architects (2018-..): articles, talks and references on interesting architecture considerations to have while building software products. Nowadays teams own the software products they build, and consequently everyone should have a role/input on the important decisions (= "architecture") related with the project. This does not mean everyone needs to be a software architect, however I argue that everyone should be mindful and aware of architecture (the important decisions).
  • Well Rounded Product Teams (2019-..): articles, talks and references on the topic of Well Rounded Product Teams (WIP).
  • Sociotechnical Architecture (2019-..): articles, talks and material on the topic of Sociotechnical Architecutre: the holistic co-design approach to technical and organizational systems, given the inherent impact they have on each other. (WIP).
