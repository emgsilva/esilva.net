ModernArch Consulting is a product that I offer in collaboration with Nick Tune to help organizations Kickstart and Accelerate their architecture modernization journeys.

We have been learning and shaping this offering for a while, after running it with multiple clients. We offer a bespoke engagement tailored to your organization specific context, challenges and opportunities around starting or accelerating the modernizing your architecture, products, and/or team structures. These modernization efforts are certainly challenging. However, we believe that with the sort of facilitation we are providing, we will help you accelerate your modernization journey and help you create long-lasting, durable change - and with that, achieve a more sustainable flow of change.
