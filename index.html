My name is Eduardo da Silva and I am (Software) Tech Leader & Sociotechnical Architect working and living in the Netherlands.
My passions are Tech Leadership and (Socio-Technical) Software Architecture and how to best use these to improve how we design, engineer and build software products and the organizations behind them. This entails technical and organizational (systems) developments and how to set them on a continuous improvement model. In this website you will mostly find articles, projects and talks related with these topics.
