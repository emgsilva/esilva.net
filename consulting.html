🧭 My mission: help (software) tech-enabled organizations to modernize and achieve a more sustainable fast flow of value & change

...so they can quickly & continuously maximize their value and respond to their increasingly fast-changing environment.

My products and services are developed with that premise in mind, and they aim to help customers achieve profound and durable improvements towards a more sustainable and fast flow of value & change.
